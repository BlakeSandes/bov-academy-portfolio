About

Here is my Site to display and promote all my web creations while studying at Bov Academy...or at least the current iteration of it. I hope I deliver in conveying my developing talents as an aspiring full stack developer. The site in its current state is leveraging my knowledge of predominantly HTML5 and CSS3, with a sprinkling of Javascript here and there.

Who am I? Well, I am a Creative type I suppose. I am a father, a married man, a woodworker, Canadian-American, Extroverted- Introvert. I love music and art, and Japanese animation. Hayao Miyazaki is my greatest inspiration. I have dabbled in the healing arts and different forms of spirituality for some time. Enough to fool people into thinking I may embody wisdom. I enjoy being out in nature very much and have worked outside most of my adult life. I am slightly weird and have a penchant for fringe thinking. Got to keep things interesting you know!

After my studies with Bov Academy I have big plans! Well, ideally work for a company building things that are actually useful and contribute towards tangible positivity, and of course have as much fun as possible in the process. I also would love to get paid well. I would love to be able to afford a house to so my girls can play in a garden of our own. You know, basic necessities. Oh, and travel. This is a pretty fantastic planet and I would love to see more of it!
